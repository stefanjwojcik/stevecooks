# Perfect Salmon Every Time

Salmon is one of the most forgiving fish to cook, packed with omega-3 fatty acids and incredible flavor. Learning proper technique ensures moist, flaky results.

## Selecting Salmon

- Look for bright, firm flesh with no fishy odor
- Wild-caught vs. farmed: both are nutritious, wild has more flavor
- Skin-on helps hold the fish together during cooking

## Cooking Methods

### Pan-Searing
The most popular method for achieving crispy skin and tender flesh.

1. Pat salmon dry and season with salt and pepper
2. Heat oil in a pan over medium-high heat
3. Place skin-side down and press gently for 30 seconds
4. Cook 4-5 minutes without moving
5. Flip and cook 2-3 minutes more

### Oven Roasting
Foolproof method for perfectly cooked salmon.

- Bake at 400°F (200°C) for 12-15 minutes
- Internal temperature should reach 145°F (63°C)

### Poaching
Gentle cooking method for delicate, moist salmon.

### Grilling
Creates beautiful char marks and smoky flavor.

## Doneness Guide

- **Medium-rare**: 120°F (49°C) - translucent center
- **Medium**: 130°F (54°C) - slightly translucent
- **Well-done**: 145°F (63°C) - opaque throughout

## Flavor Pairings

- Lemon and dill (classic)
- Soy, ginger, and garlic (Asian-inspired)
- Honey and mustard glaze
- Mediterranean herbs and olive oil
